# Signo Artist Terms

## Transit insurance

For artworks valued over $500 AUD, artists are strongly encouraged to send with AusPost Extra Cover or comparable carrier insurance.

Signo holds buyer payments in escrow until delivery is confirmed. If a buyer raises a dispute for transit damage:

- **Insured shipments**: The artist can claim the loss through their carrier. The buyer is refunded from escrow.
- **Uninsured shipments**: The artist bears the cost of the refund. Signo does not cover transit damage from uninsured shipments.

The cost of carrier insurance is small relative to the value of the work and protects artists against significant financial loss.

Artists acknowledge this responsibility when confirming dispatch through the Signo platform.
